# POS Product Team, H2 2026 Performance System

Owner: Kurt Molina. Built from the QuantumLight (Revolut) playbook and two
alignment calls with Ivan Grytsenko (29 Jun and the Jul call). Number KPIs are
placeholders until set bottom-up with management.

## The measurement model (settled on the Jul call)

- The north star stays absolute PBT in pesos per line (QRPh, Online,
  Moto/Deals, Credit Line, Solar, Offline), PBT = Volume x Profitability %,
  with the Tier 2 diagnostics (margin, risk, opex) and the phase overlay.
- Line volumes and PBT are management-owned numbers. The product team is not
  scored or punished on them ("they are not magicians"). Sales-style volume
  KPIs belong to the network team; the product team exists to balance for
  profit, and sometimes less sales means more profit.
- What PMs own and are scored on is delivery of the agreed projects:
  - Speed: delivered on time, yes or no, or faster.
  - Quality: delivered at the level expected, thought through, minimal
    resyncs ("if I have to resync and come back again and again, the quality
    goes down automatically").
- The bar is seniority, not peers: "I am not comparing you with him. I am
  comparing your bar with your performance." Juniors get a mistake discount
  and are grown with projects; non-juniors must arrive fully thought through
  (Elias carries a senior superstar bar, domain knowledge the only discount).
- Initiative is the third dimension, assessed for growth, not bonus: does the
  person know their numbers cold and connect problems to actions, bringing
  initiatives assessed easy vs difficult and high vs low impact.

## Access model

- The Executive Project Tracker is shared with the whole team: everyone sees
  what everyone is doing, one centralized view.
- The Delivery Scoreboard (scores, initiative log, calibration) is Kurt-only.

## Cadence

- Weekly (Monday standup): tracker walk; set Due dates when steps are agreed;
  update Delivered and Resyncs; collect initiatives into the log.
- Monthly: KPI readout on the KPI Targets H2 tab (context, not scoring), fed
  by Nhat's PBT model and Jenny's dashboard.
- Quarterly: QuantumLight Product Owner scorecard per PM against the seniority
  talent bar. First run is RETROACTIVE, covering the past 3 months, done now
  rather than waiting for December (Ivan). Output lands in the Calibration tab
  as one score per person (5 to 1); compare to gut feel, then adopt.
- Semi-annual: promotions on sustained A-player grades, pay to benchmark.
